Preventive Maintenance Protocols for Remote Sites

Unplanned breakdowns during exploration runs quickly exhaust project budgets. Implementing rigorous preventative steps preserves high-torque mechanical parts and extends system life.

Hydraulic Oil Management and Filtration

High ambient heat combined with dust can break down hydraulic oils, causing system wear. Regular filter replacements and fluid analysis keep hydraulic pressures within optimal operational ranges.
